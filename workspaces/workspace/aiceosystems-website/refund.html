Refund Policy

30-day money-back guarantee on all our products

Last updated: March 29, 2024

1. 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

At AI CEO Systems, we are confident in the quality and effectiveness of our products. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all one-time purchase products. If you are not satisfied with your purchase for any reason, you may request a full refund within 30 days of the purchase date.

2. Eligibility for Refunds

To be eligible for a refund under our 30-day money-back guarantee:

  • The refund request must be made within 30 days of the original purchase date
  • The product must be a one-time purchase (not a subscription)
  • You must provide proof of purchase (order number or receipt)
  • You must demonstrate that you have attempted to use the product and found it unsuitable for your needs

3. Non-Refundable Items

The following items are not eligible for refunds:

  • Monthly or annual subscription payments (after the initial 30-day period)
  • Custom development or consulting services
  • Products purchased more than 30 days ago
  • Products that have been significantly modified or customized
  • Third-party products or services integrated with our platform

4. Subscription Cancellations

For monthly or annual subscriptions:

  • You may cancel your subscription at any time
  • Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing cycle
  • No refunds will be provided for partial billing periods
  • You will retain access to the service until the end of your current billing period

5. Refund Process

To request a refund:

  1. Contact our support team at support@aiceosystems.digital within 30 days of purchase
  2. Provide your order number, purchase date, and reason for the refund request
  3. Allow up to 5 business days for us to review your request
  4. If approved, refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days

6. Refund Method

Refunds will be issued using the same payment method used for the original purchase. If the original payment method is no longer available, we will work with you to arrange an alternative refund method.

7. Processing Fees

All refunds are subject to applicable processing fees charged by our payment processor (Stripe). These fees are typically 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction and will be deducted from your refund amount.

8. Product Access After Refund

Upon approval of a refund:

  • Your access to the product will be immediately revoked
  • You must delete any downloaded materials or software
  • All licenses and rights granted under the purchase will be terminated
  • You may not use any content or materials obtained from the product

9. Abuse of Refund Policy

We reserve the right to deny refund requests if we believe there is abuse of our refund policy, including but not limited to:

  • Multiple refund requests from the same customer
  • Refund requests made after significant use of the product
  • Refund requests without legitimate reasons
  • Any fraudulent activity related to purchases or refunds

10. Changes to Refund Policy

We may update or modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Your continued use of our products after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.

12. Good Faith Guarantee

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you have any issues or concerns, please contact us first before requesting a refund. We are committed to working with you to resolve any problems and ensure you get the most value from our products.

Stripe Payment Processing

All refunds are processed through Stripe, our secure payment processor. Stripe handles all aspects of payment processing and refund transactions in accordance with their policies and procedures.

By requesting a refund, you acknowledge that the refund will be processed through Stripe and may be subject to Stripe's processing fees and timelines.
