## AUTOMATION WORKFLOWS

### Gumroad Sales Funnel Automation
```
1. Lead Magnet Download → Mailchimp Integration
   Trigger: Email capture on landing page
   Action: Send free report + welcome sequence

2. Product Purchase → Customer Segmentation
   Trigger: Gumroad purchase
   Action: Tag customer, send onboarding sequence

3. Abandoned Cart Recovery
   Trigger: Cart abandonment (24 hours)
   Action: Send reminder email with 10% discount

4. Post-Purchase Upsell
   Trigger: 7 days after purchase
   Action: Offer complementary product at 20% off
```

### Fiverr Order Management
```
1. New Order → Provider Notification
   Trigger: Fiverr order received
   Action: Auto-forward to vetted provider

2. Delivery Reminder
   Trigger: 2 days before deadline
   Action: Send reminder to provider

3. Quality Check
   Trigger: Provider delivers work
   Action: Review against checklist, approve/revise

4. Client Delivery
   Trigger: Quality check passed
   Action: Deliver to client with thank you message
```

## CUSTOMER SERVICE SCRIPTS

### Common Inquiry Responses
```
"What's your experience in Singapore real estate?"
→ "I've been actively investing and consulting in Singapore property for 10+ years, with expertise in residential, commercial, and industrial sectors. I've helped over 100 clients achieve their investment goals."

"Do you offer refunds?"
→ "I stand behind my products with a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied, I'll provide a full refund - no questions asked."

"Can you customize this service?"
→ "Absolutely! I can tailor any service to your specific needs. Let's schedule a quick call to discuss your requirements and I'll provide a custom quote."

"How quickly can you deliver?"
→ "Standard delivery is [X] days, but I offer expedited service for urgent projects. Let me know your timeline and I'll do my best to accommodate."
```

### Issue Resolution
```
Late Delivery:
"I apologize for the delay. I'm committed to delivering quality work and want to ensure you get the best results. I can offer a 20% discount for the inconvenience, and I'll have this to you within 24 hours."

Quality Concerns:
"I want to make sure you're completely satisfied. Could you share specific areas you'd like me to improve? I'll revise the work at no additional cost until it meets your expectations."

Technical Issues:
"I'm experiencing a technical issue that's affecting delivery. I'm working to resolve this immediately and will keep you updated every step of the way. As an apology, I'd like to offer you a complimentary upgrade."
```
