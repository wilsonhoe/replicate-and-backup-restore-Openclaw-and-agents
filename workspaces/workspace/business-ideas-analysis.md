**Trend:**
- SMEs need leads but lack systems
- AI outreach replacing manual sales

**Business Model:**
Generate leads → sell to businesses OR charge monthly retainer

**OpenClaw Fit: STRONG ✅**
- ✅ Browser automation + scraping
- ✅ No paid API needed
- ✅ Can use Gmail automation for outreach

**Execution:**
1. Scrape business directories / LinkedIn (browser)
2. Enrich data (emails, websites via scraping)
3. Auto-send outreach (Gmail automation)
4. Track responses (spreadsheet/CRM)
5. Deliver qualified leads to clients

**Monetization:**
- Retainer: $300–$2,000/month per client
- Pay-per-lead: $5-50 per qualified lead
- Commission on closed deals

**Startup Costs:** $0
**Time to Revenue:** 1-2 weeks (find first client)
**Scalability:** MEDIUM - requires client management
