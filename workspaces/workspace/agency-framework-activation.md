## 🏢 Agency Coordination Protocol

### **Daily Stand-up Schedule (08:30 AM)**
**Duration**: 15 minutes
**Format**: Rapid status updates and coordination
**Lead**: AI CEO (Lisa)

**Agenda**:
1. **Previous Day Results** (2 min each agent)
   - Key achievements and metrics
   - Challenges encountered
   - Revenue generated

2. **Today's Priorities** (1 min each agent)
   - Primary focus areas
   - Expected deliverables
   - Resource requirements

3. **Cross-Agent Coordination** (5 min)
   - Content → Lead Generation handoffs
   - Product → Sales alignment
   - Analytics → All agents insights

### **Weekly Strategy Review (Fridays 2:00 PM)**
**Duration**: 60 minutes
**Focus**: Performance analysis and strategic adjustment

**Agenda**:
1. **Revenue Performance Review** (20 min)
   - Total revenue against $1K/month target
   - Individual agent contribution analysis
   - ROI assessment by activity type

2. **Customer Feedback Integration** (15 min)
   - Customer satisfaction metrics
   - Product improvement opportunities
   - Market trend analysis

3. **Strategy Refinement** (20 min)
   - Successful tactic identification
   - Underperforming area analysis
   - Next week priority adjustments

4. **Resource Allocation** (5 min)
   - Agent workload balancing
   - Tool and platform optimization
   - Budget allocation recommendations

### **Quality Assurance Protocol**
**AI CEO Validation Required For**:
- All external communications (emails, social posts)
- Revenue-generating activities (pricing, sales offers)
- Customer-facing content (courses, templates)
- Partnership agreements and contracts
- Strategic pivots and major changes

**Agent Autonomy Levels**:
- **Level 1**: Research and analysis (full autonomy)
- **Level 2**: Content creation (review required)
- **Level 3**: Customer interaction (approval required)
- **Level 4**: Revenue activities (CEO validation required)
