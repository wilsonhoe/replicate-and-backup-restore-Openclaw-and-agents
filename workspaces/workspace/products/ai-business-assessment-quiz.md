## Scoring System:

**Automation Readiness Score (0-100)**:
- Mostly a) = Low readiness (0-25)
- Mostly b) = Basic readiness (25-50)
- Mostly c) = Good readiness (50-75)
- Mostly d) = High readiness (75-100)

**Time Savings Potential**:
- Low: 5-10 hours/week ($500-1K value)
- Medium: 10-25 hours/week ($1-2.5K value)
- High: 25-40 hours/week ($2.5-4K value)
- Very High: 40+ hours/week ($4K+ value)

**ROI Projection**:
- Based on current costs (staff, tools, time)
- Based on revenue potential
- 30-day / 90-day / 180-day projections

**Priority Recommendations**:
- Top 5 automations for immediate impact
- Quick wins (1-week implementation)
- Medium-term (30-day implementation)
- Long-term (90-day transformation)
