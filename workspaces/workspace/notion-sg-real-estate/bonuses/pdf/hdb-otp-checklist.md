# HDB OTP Checklist
## Complete Guide to HDB Resale Process in Singapore

---

## Phase 1: Before OTP (Offer to Purchase)

### Step 1: Buyer Qualification ✅

**Check Buyer's Eligibility:**
- [ ] Citizenship/PR status verified
- [ ] Age (21+ for singles, 21+ for couples under Fiance/Fiancee Scheme)
- [ ] Income ceiling check (if applying for HDB loan)
- [ ] CPF usage calculated
- [ ] Previous property ownership checked (30-month rule)
- [ ] HDB loan eligibility letter obtained (if applicable)

**Documents Required:**
- [ ] NRIC (both buyer and seller)
- [ ] Marriage certificate (if applicable)
- [ ] CPF statements (latest 3 months)
- [ ] Income documents (payslips, tax returns)
- [ ] Bank loan approval letter (if taking bank loan)

---

### Step 2: Property Valuation ✅

- [ ] Request HDB valuation (if taking HDB loan)
- [ ] Obtain independent valuation (if needed)
- [ ] Compare valuation with asking price
- [ ] Calculate Cash Over Valuation (COV)

---

### Step 3: Negotiation ✅

- [ ] Agree on purchase price
- [ ] Agree on OTP amount (typically $1,000-$5,000)
- [ ] Set OTP exercise date (typically 14-21 days)
- [ ] Discuss inclusion of fittings/fixtures
- [ ] Confirm timeline for completion

---

## Phase 2: OTP Issuance

### Step 4: Prepare OTP Document ✅

**Required in OTP:**
- [ ] Property address
- [ ] Agreed purchase price
- [ ] OTP amount ($1,000-$5,000)
- [ ] Option exercise date (must be specified)
- [ ] Completion date (typically 8-10 weeks from OTP exercise)
- [ ] Special conditions (if any)

**Sample OTP Timeline:**
```
Day 0:    OTP issued, buyer pays $1,000-$5,000
Day 1-14: Buyer decides whether to exercise
Day 14:   OTP exercise deadline
Day 15:   Exercise OTP, pay 5% down payment
Week 8-10: Completion at HDB
```

---

### Step 5: OTP Payment ✅

- [ ] Collect OTP amount ($1,000-$5,000)
- [ ] Issue receipt
- [ ] Provide copy of OTP to buyer
- [ ] Retain seller's copy
- [ ] Record in CRM

---

## Phase 3: OTP Exercise Period (14-21 Days)

### Step 6: Buyer Due Diligence ✅

**Buyer should:**
- [ ] Arrange for bank loan (if applicable)
- [ ] Calculate total costs (down payment, stamp duty, legal fees)
- [ ] Review property condition
- [ ] Check for outstanding maintenance fees
- [ ] Verify seller's HDB ownership documents

**Seller should:**
- [ ] Prepare HDB ownership documents
- [ ] Clear outstanding maintenance fees
- [ ] Ensure no illegal additions/renovations
- [ ] Prepare for inspection (if any)

---

### Step 7: Exercise OTP ✅

**When Buyer Exercises OTP:**

- [ ] Buyer signs OTP exercise form
- [ ] Buyer pays remaining down payment (5% total minus OTP)
  - Cash: 5% of purchase price
  - CPF: Up to available CPF Ordinary Account balance
- [ ] Issue receipt for balance payment
- [ ] Return original OTP document (stamped "Exercised")
- [ ] Record in CRM: OTP Exercised

**Payment Breakdown Example ($500,000 flat):**
```
OTP paid:              $1,000-$5,000
Exercise payment:      Balance to reach 5% ($25,000 - OTP)
Total down payment:    $25,000 (5%)
Remaining 95%:         $475,000 (HDB loan/Bank loan)
```

---

## Phase 4: Resale Application

### Step 8: Submit Resale Application ✅

**Timeline: Within 1 week of OTP exercise**

**Seller submits:**
- [ ] Resale application via HDB Resale Portal
- [ ] Upload OTP (exercised)
- [ ] Upload seller's NRIC
- [ ] Upload marriage certificate (if applicable)
- [ ] Pay administrative fee ($80 for 4-room and larger)

**Buyer submits:**
- [ ] Resale application via HDB Resale Portal
- [ ] Upload OTP (exercised)
- [ ] Upload buyer's NRIC
- [ ] Upload marriage certificate (if applicable)
- [ ] Upload loan approval letter (if bank loan)
- [ ] Pay administrative fee ($80 for 4-room and larger)

---

### Step 9: HDB Processing ✅

**HDB Review (typically 2-4 weeks):**
- [ ] HDB reviews resale application
- [ ] HDB verifies eligibility of both parties
- [ ] HDB conducts valuation (if not done)
- [ ] HDB schedules first appointment

**You will receive:**
- [ ] SMS/email notification of HDB Acceptance Letter
- [ ] First appointment date (typically 4-6 weeks after application)

---

## Phase 5: First Appointment (HDB)

### Step 10: Prepare for First Appointment ✅

**Seller brings:**
- [ ] Original NRIC
- [ ] Marriage certificate (if applicable)
- [ ] Original HDB ownership documents
- [ ] Outstanding loan statement (if any)
- [ ] Fire insurance policy (if any)

**Buyer brings:**
- [ ] Original NRIC
- [ ] Marriage certificate (if applicable)
- [ ] CPF withdrawal forms
- [ ] Bank loan documents (if applicable)
- [ ] Cashier's order for down payment (if any)

---

### Step 11: First Appointment at HDB ✅

**What happens:**
- [ ] Identity verification
- [ ] Review of resale terms
- [ ] Sign resale documents
- [ ] Buyer pays stamp duty (if not paid)
- [ ] Arrange for fire insurance (if applicable)
- [ ] Schedule second appointment (typically 6-8 weeks later)

---

## Phase 6: Between Appointments

### Step 12: Legal Completion ✅

**Buyer's lawyer:**
- [ ] Prepares mortgage documents
- [ ] Arranges for CPF withdrawal
- [ ] Prepares bank loan disbursement
- [ ] Conducts title search

**Seller's obligations:**
- [ ] Clear outstanding maintenance fees
- [ ] Maintain property condition
- [ ] Cancel existing fire insurance (if any)

---

## Phase 7: Second Appointment (Completion)

### Step 13: Final Appointment at HDB ✅

**What happens:**
- [ ] Handover of keys
- [ ] Final payment settlement
- [ ] Sign completion documents
- [ ] Transfer of ownership completed
- [ ] Seller receives sale proceeds
- [ ] Buyer receives keys and ownership

**Seller receives:**
- [ ] Sale proceeds (minus outstanding loan, if any)
- [ ] Refund of CPF (with accrued interest)
- [ ] Receipt for transaction

**Buyer receives:**
- [ ] Keys to the flat
- [ ] Ownership documents
- [ ] Receipt for transaction

---

## Phase 8: Post-Completion

### Step 14: Follow-up ✅

**After completion:**
- [ ] Update your CRM: Deal status = Closed
- [ ] Record commission earned
- [ ] Request testimonial from satisfied client
- [ ] Follow up for referrals
- [ ] Add lesson learned to Knowledge Base

## Cost Summary (Example: $500,000 HDB)

**Buyer Costs:**
- Down payment (5%): $25,000
- Stamp duty: $9,600 (first $180k: 1%, next $180k: 2%, remaining: 3%)
- Legal fees: ~$500-$800
- Agent commission: 1% of price = $5,000
- Total: ~$40,000+

**Seller Costs:**
- Agent commission: 2% of price = $10,000
- Outstanding maintenance fees (if any)
- Early loan repayment fee (if applicable)

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Don't**: Issue OTP without confirming buyer's eligibility
❌ **Don't**: Let OTP expire without follow-up
❌ **Don't**: Submit incomplete resale application
❌ **Don't**: Miss HDB appointment dates
❌ **Don't**: Forget to record deal in CRM after closing

✅ **Do**: Keep all documents organized
✅ **Do**: Set reminders for key dates
✅ **Do**: Communicate proactively with clients
✅ **Do**: Follow up promptly after completion
