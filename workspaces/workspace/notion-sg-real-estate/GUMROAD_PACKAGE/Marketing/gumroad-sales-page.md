## See What's Inside

### The 5-Database System

<img src="screenshots/dashboard-preview.png" alt="SG Property Pro Dashboard" width="100%">

**1. Leads Database**
- Name, contact, budget range
- District preference (D1-28)
- NEL zone interest flag
- Source tracking (Referral, PropertyGuru, Facebook, etc.)
- Status: New → Contacted → Qualified → Active → Closed Lost

**2. Properties Database**
- Address with district auto-tagging
- Property type: HDB (BTO/Resale), Condo, Landed
- Size (sqft), price, PSF calculation
- OTP dates: Option Fee Paid, Exercise Date, Completion
- Commission: Buyer 2%, Seller 1%

**3. Deals Database**
- Linked to Lead + Property
- Stage: Prospecting → Viewing → Negotiation → OTP → Closing → Commission Received
- Probability % for weighted pipeline
- Commission breakdown (gross, split, net)
- Expected close date

**4. Activities Database**
- Call, viewing, follow-up, meeting
- Linked to leads/deals
- Due dates and reminders
- Notes and outcomes

**5. Knowledge Base**
- Scripts (first call, viewing, closing)
- Objection handling
- Lessons learned from past deals
- Market insights
