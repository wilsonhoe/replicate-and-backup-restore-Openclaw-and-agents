# SG Property Pro - Pro Edition ($27)

**The complete Singapore real estate CRM system.**

## What's Included

Perfect for active agents managing leads, properties, activities, and deals with full commission tracking.

### Databases (5)

#### 1. Leads Database
- Contact info (name, phone, email)
- Source tracking (Door Knocking, Referral, Online, Social Media, Walk-in)
- Property interest (HDB, Condo, EC, Landed)
- Budget range tracking
- Status pipeline (New → Contacted → Viewing → Offer → Closed/Lost)
- Priority levels (Hot/Warm/Cold)
- Last contact & next follow-up dates

#### 2. Properties Database
- Property details (address, type, district)
- NEL zone areas (Punggol, Sengkang, Hougang, Serangoon, CBD)
- Price & **automatic PSF calculations** (formula)
- Size, bedrooms
- Status tracking (Available, Under Offer, Sold, Off Market)
- Seller contact info

#### 3. Activities Database
- Track door knocking sessions
- Cold call logs
- Viewing appointments
- Follow-up tracking
- Meeting notes
- Research activities
- Outcome tracking (Lead Generated, Callback, No Answer, Not Interested, Market Intel)
- Leads generated counter
- Conversation duration tracking
- Auto-add to Knowledge Base checkbox

#### 4. Deals Database
- Complete deal pipeline with Singapore HDB OTP workflow
- Deal types (HDB→HDB, HDB→Private, Private→Private)
- Price tracking
- Commission % with **automatic calculations**
- **Estimated Commission** (formula: Price × Commission %)
- **Probability weighting** (10%-100%)
- **Weighted Commission** (formula: Est. Commission × Probability)
- OTP tracking (Date, Exercise Date)
- Resale application stages
- HDB acceptance tracking
- Next action & due date

#### 5. Knowledge Base Database
- Door knocking scripts
- Cold call scripts
- Objection handling
- Follow-up email templates
- Closing scripts
- Market insights
- Lessons learned
- Success rate tracking
- Tagging (HDB, Condo, EC, Landed, First-time buyer)
