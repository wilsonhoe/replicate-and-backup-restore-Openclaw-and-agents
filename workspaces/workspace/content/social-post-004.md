## LinkedIn Version

Most solopreneurs try to automate everything.

Bad strategy.

**80% of automation value comes from just 20% of workflows.**

The Pareto Principle, named after economist Vilfredo Pareto, shows that a small portion of inputs drives the majority of results (Taskade, 2024).

**The 5 Automation MVPs:**

1. **Email Filtering** — Auto-sort, auto-reply, auto-archive
2. **Calendar Scheduling** — No more back-and-forth emails
3. **Invoice Generation** — Bill clients on autopilot
4. **Lead Scoring** — Focus on hot prospects only
5. **Customer Follow-ups** — Never lose a sale to silence

Master these five first. Then expand.

Research shows that professionals who focus 20% of their time on high-impact activities have roughly half the burnout rate of those who don't (Taskade, 2024).

Which of these five would give you the most time back?
