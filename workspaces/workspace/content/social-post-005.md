## LinkedIn Version

**From 60 hours to 20 hours: An AI Automation Blueprint**

*This is a composite example based on reported automation outcomes from multiple sources (Digital Applied, 2026; OpenAI case studies, 2025).*

**Automation Stack:**

**Content Creation (ChatGPT + Claude)**
- Before: 15 hrs/week writing
- After: 3 hrs/week prompting
- Savings: 12 hrs

**Workflow Automation (Zapier + Make)**
- Before: 10 hrs/week manual tasks
- After: 1 hr/week monitoring
- Savings: 9 hrs

**Project Management (Notion AI)**
- Before: 8 hrs/week organizing
- After: 2 hrs/week updating
- Savings: 6 hrs

**Scheduling (Calendly + Zoom)**
- Before: 5 hrs/week coordinating
- After: 30 min/week managing
- Savings: 4.5 hrs

**Customer Support (Intercom AI)**
- Before: 12 hrs/week answering
- After: 2 hrs/week supervising
- Savings: 10 hrs

**Total: 41.5 hours/week saved**

The key insight: Don't automate everything. Automate the 20% that gives 80% of value (Pareto Principle; Taskade, 2024).

What would you do with an extra 40 hours?
