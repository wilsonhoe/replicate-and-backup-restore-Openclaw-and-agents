## LinkedIn Version

Choosing between Zapier, Make, and n8n for your automation stack?

Here's my breakdown after testing all three:

**Zapier**
- 7,000+ app integrations (Digital Applied, 2026)
- Easiest setup—most users build first workflow in 15 minutes
- Best for: Beginners, quick wins
- Price: Free tier (100 tasks), then $20/mo

**Make (formerly Integromat)**
- Visual canvas-based workflow builder
- 60% cheaper than Zapier at equivalent volume (Digital Applied, 2026)
- Best for: Power users, complex branching logic
- Price: Free tier (1,000 ops), then $9/mo

**n8n**
- Self-hosted option—complete data control
- Free tier unlimited workflows (self-hosted)
- Best for: Privacy-conscious, developers, AI agent workflows
- Price: $0 self-hosted, or $20/mo cloud

My recommendation: Start with Zapier, graduate to Make when you hit limits.
