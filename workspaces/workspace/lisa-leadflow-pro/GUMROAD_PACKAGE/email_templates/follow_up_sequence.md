# LeadFlow Pro - Email Templates

## Welcome Sequence for Real Estate Leads

### Email 1: Initial Contact (Day 0)

**Subject:** Quick question about your property search

Hi {{first_name}},

I noticed you're looking for properties in {{city}}. I'm {{agent_name}}, a local real estate specialist.

I help buyers find their perfect home without the stress. I have access to:
- Off-market listings
- Properties before they hit MLS
- Exclusive neighborhood insights

Would you like me to send you a curated list of properties that match your criteria?

Best regards,
{{agent_name}}
{{phone}}
{{email}}

---

### Email 2: Value Add (Day 2)

**Subject:** {{city}} market report - trends you should know

Hi {{first_name}},

I put together a quick market update for {{city}} that I think you'll find valuable:

**Key trends this month:**
- Average days on market: {{avg_days}}
- Price per sq ft trending: {{trend}}
- Inventory level: {{inventory}}

[Link to full report]

Let me know if you'd like to discuss how this affects your search.

{{agent_name}}

---

### Email 3: Social Proof (Day 5)

**Subject:** How I helped {{testimonial_name}} find their home

Hi {{first_name}},

I wanted to share a quick story. {{testimonial_name}} was looking in {{city}} for {{duration}} without success.

We connected, and within {{timeframe}}, they closed on their dream home at {{price_point}}.

Here's what they said:
"{{testimonial_quote}}"

If you're feeling frustrated with your search, I'd love to help.

{{agent_name}}

### Email 4: Urgency/Scarcity (Day 7)

**Subject:** New listing alert: {{address}}

Hi {{first_name}},

A new property just came on the market in {{neighborhood}} that matches your criteria:

- {{bedrooms}} bed / {{bathrooms}} bath
- {{sqft}} sq ft
- Listed at ${{price}}

Properties in this area typically sell within {{days}} days.

Want to schedule a viewing?

{{agent_name}}
{{phone}}

---

### Email 5: Breakup Email (Day 14)

**Subject:** Should I close your file?

Hi {{first_name}},

I haven't heard back, so I'm assuming you've either:
1. Found a property (congrats!)
2. Paused your search
3. Working with another agent

No worries either way - just let me know so I can update my records.

If you'd still like help, reply and I'll keep sending you the best listings.

Otherwise, good luck with your search!

{{agent_name}}
