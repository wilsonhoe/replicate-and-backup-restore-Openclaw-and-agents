## Tier 1: Immediate Applications (Highest Payout)

### 1. Shopify Plus Affiliate
- **Commission:** $2,000 per referral (one-time)
- **Recurring:** No (but high volume potential)
- **Requirements:** Active website, audience in e-commerce/business niche
- **Application:** https://www.shopify.com/partners/affiliate-program
- **Content Angle:** "How to Scale from Shopify to Shopify Plus"
- **Estimated Conversion:** 2-3 referrals/month = $4K-$6K

### 2. HubSpot Affiliate Program
- **Commission:** Up to $1,000 per customer (tiered)
- **Recurring:** 30% recurring for first year
- **Requirements:** Content site, business/marketing audience
- **Application:** https://www.hubspot.com/partners/affiliate
- **Content Angle:** "Best CRM for Small Business" comparisons
- **Estimated Conversion:** 3-5 referrals/month = $3K-$5K + recurring

### 3. Semrush Affiliate Program
- **Commission:** $200 per sale + $10 per trial
- **Recurring:** Yes (ongoing commissions)
- **Requirements:** SEO/marketing focused content
- **Application:** https://www.semrush.com/affiliates/
- **Content Angle:** "SEO Tools Comparison", "How to Do Keyword Research"
- **Estimated Conversion:** 10-15 sales/month = $2K-$3K + recurring
