## Application Tips

### Before Applying:
1. **Have a website or social presence** - Even if small, show you're serious
2. **Create relevant content first** - Write 1-2 articles mentioning the tool
3. **Be honest about traffic** - Quality > quantity for affiliate managers
4. **Show you understand their product** - Mention specific features

### If Rejected:
1. Ask for feedback
2. Build more content and reapply in 30-60 days
3. Consider alternative affiliate networks (PartnerStack, Impact, ShareASale)

### After Approval:
1. Get your affiliate links immediately
2. Update existing content with affiliate links
3. Track clicks and conversions
4. Request marketing assets (banners, email swipes)
5. Build relationship with affiliate manager
