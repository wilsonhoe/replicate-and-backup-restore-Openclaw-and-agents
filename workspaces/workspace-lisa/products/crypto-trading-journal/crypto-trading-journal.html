🧮 P&L Calculations

Long Position: P&L = (Exit Price - Entry Price) × Quantity - Entry Fees - Exit Fees

Short Position: P&L = (Entry Price - Exit Price) × Quantity - Entry Fees - Exit Fees

P&L %: (P&L / Position Size) × 100

Position Size: Entry Price × Quantity × Leverage

Trading Rules & Checklist

✅ Pre-Trade Checklist

Market trend aligns with trade direction
Clear entry trigger identified
Stop loss level defined
Take profit target set (min 2:1 R:R)
Position size calculated (max 2% risk)
No major news events pending
Emotional state is calm and focused

🚫 Trading Rules (Never Break)

⚠️
Rule #1: Risk Management

Never risk more than 2% of capital on a single trade

⚠️
Rule #2: Stop Loss

Always use a stop loss. Never move it further away.

⚠️
Rule #3: No Revenge Trading

After a loss, step away. Don't chase losses.

⚠️
Rule #4: Follow the Plan

If it's not in your trading plan, don't do it.

📈 Risk Management Calculator

Position Size: (Account Size × Risk %) / (Entry Price - Stop Loss)

Risk Amount: Account Size × Risk %

R:R Ratio: (Take Profit - Entry) / (Entry - Stop Loss)

Example: $10,000 account, 2% risk, entry $100, stop $95

Position Size = ($10,000 × 0.02) / ($100 - $95) = $200 / $5 = 40 shares
