## 🏷️ Psychological Pricing Tactics

### 1. Anchoring
- Show "Regular Price: ~~$29~~" next to "$24"
- Creates perception of value and savings

### 2. Price Ending
- Use $24 instead of $25 (left-digit effect)
- $24 feels significantly cheaper than $25

### 3. Comparison Framing
- "Less than the cost of one bad trade"
- "Cheaper than 2 cups of coffee per month" (if subscription)

### 4. Urgency (Limited Use)
- "Launch price ends Sunday"
- "First 50 customers only"
- Don't overuse or it loses credibility
