## CAPTURE METHODS

### Method 1: Built-in Screenshot (Mac)
- Cmd+Shift+4 for selected area
- Cmd+Shift+5 for full control
- Saves to Desktop

### Method 2: Built-in Screenshot (Windows)
- Win+Shift+S for snipping tool
- Print Screen for full screen
- Saves to Clipboard or Pictures

### Method 3: Browser Extension
**Awesome Screenshot** (Chrome/Firefox)
- Full page capture
- Annotation tools
- Direct save

**Nimbus Screenshot** (Chrome)
- Scrolling capture
- Video recording option
- Cloud storage

### Method 4: Dedicated App
**CleanShot X** (Mac) - $29
- Scrolling screenshots
- Annotation
- Cloud sharing

**Snagit** (Mac/Windows) - $50
- Professional features
- Video capture
- Templates

## EDITING TIPS

### Tools:
- **Free:** Canva, Photopea, GIMP
- **Paid:** Photoshop, Affinity Photo
- **Quick:** Built-in Photos app

### Edits to Make:
1. **Crop** to focus on content
2. **Adjust brightness/contrast** for clarity
3. **Add subtle drop shadow** for depth
4. **Uniform borders** if needed
5. **Text overlays** (optional, use sparingly)

### What NOT to Do:
- ❌ Heavy filters or effects
- ❌ Cluttered text overlays
- ❌ Misleading enhancements
- ❌ Blurring sensitive info (use sample data instead)

---

## COMPOSITION RULES

### Rule of Thirds
- Place key info at intersection points
- Don't center everything

### Leading Lines
- Use table rows to guide eye
- Natural flow top to bottom

### White Space
- Don't overcrowd
- Leave breathing room

### Consistency
- Same browser window size
- Same zoom level (100%)
- Same lighting/mode
