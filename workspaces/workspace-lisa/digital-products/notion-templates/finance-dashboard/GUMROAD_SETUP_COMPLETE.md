## 🆘 TROUBLESHOOTING

**Q: Gumroad won't let me publish**
A: Make sure you've connected a payment method (Stripe) and verified your identity.

**Q: Notion page won't duplicate**
A: Make sure "Publish to web" is ON. Add `?duplicate=true` to URL.

**Q: Customers can't access files**
A: Check that files are uploaded to the product (not just linked).

**Q: Stripe payment failed**
A: Verify bank account details. Contact Stripe support if needed.

**Q: No sales after launch**
A: Increase promotion. Post in more communities. Consider discount code for first 10 customers.
