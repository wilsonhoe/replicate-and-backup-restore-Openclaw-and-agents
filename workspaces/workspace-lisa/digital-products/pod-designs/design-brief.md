# POD Design Brief - Digital Nomad & Productivity Niche

## Design Style
- Minimalist
- Clean typography
- Neutral color palette (black, white, beige, sage green)
- Modern sans-serif fonts

## Product Categories
1. **T-Shirts** - $25-35 retail
2. **Hoodies** - $45-65 retail
3. **Mugs** - $15-25 retail
4. **Tote Bags** - $20-30 retail
5. **Phone Cases** - $25-35 retail
6. **Stickers** - $5-10 retail
