## Content Production Workflow

### Step 1: Script Writing (30 min/video)
**Template:**
```
Hook (0-15 sec): "This AI tool just changed everything..."
Intro (15-30 sec): What we're covering + why it matters
Main Content (3-8 min): Tool demo, features, use cases
Pros/Cons (30 sec): Honest assessment
CTA (15 sec): Subscribe + affiliate link mention
```

**Tools:** ChatGPT/Claude for first draft, manual editing for personality

### Step 2: Voiceover (10 min/video)
**Tool:** ElevenLabs
**Voice:** Choose warm, professional male/female voice
**Settings:** Stability 50%, Clarity 75%, Style Exaggeration 20%
**Output:** MP3, 44.1kHz, normalized

### Step 3: Visual Assembly (20-30 min/video)
**Option A: Pictory**
- Upload script + voiceover
- AI matches stock footage
- Auto-captions
- Export 1080p

**Option B: Manual Editing (CapCut/Premiere)**
- Screen recordings (OBS)
- Stock footage (Pexels, Pixabay - free)
- B-roll transitions
- Text overlays
- Export 1080p

### Step 4: Thumbnail (10 min/video)
**Tool:** Canva
**Template:** Bold text + contrasting colors + face/object
**Formula:** 3-5 words max, high contrast, curiosity gap

### Step 5: Upload & Optimize (10 min/video)
- Title: Include keyword + curiosity (60 chars max)
- Description: 200+ words, keywords, timestamps, affiliate links
- Tags: 10-15 relevant tags
- Cards: Link to related videos
- End screen: Subscribe + next video

## Growth Strategy

### Months 1-2: Foundation
- Upload 3 videos/week (12 videos/month)
- Focus on SEO optimization
- Build initial subscriber base (0-1K)
- Engage with every comment

### Months 3-4: Acceleration
- Increase to 5 videos/week
- Collaborate with similar channels
- Run YouTube ads on best performers
- Hit monetization threshold (1K subs, 4K hours)

### Months 5-6: Monetization
- Enable AdSense (~$2K-5K/month at 100K views)
- Activate affiliate links in descriptions
- Pitch sponsorships ($500-2K per video)
- Launch own product (template, course)
