# Nyx Research Learnings

## Research Date: 2026-04-09

### Key Market Insights

#### 1. AI Agent Market Opportunity
- Market size: $7.8-10.9B (2026) → $52B by 2030 (43% CAGR)
- Vertical AI agents grow 2-3x faster than horizontal tools
- Real estate qualifier specifically cited as high-demand vertical ($499-799/mo licensing)
- Implementation gap (48/100 score) = massive service opportunity
- Custom agency builds: $25K-$100K; licensing model undercuts by 10x

#### 2. Singapore SME AI Adoption
- Adoption tripled from 4.2% to 14.5% in one year
- Implementation score: only 48/100 (massive gap)
- Government subsidies (PSG, SkillsFuture, ECI) cover 70-90% of costs
- Top barriers: compliance (27%), skills gap (24%), cost (15%)
- Market has 10x more demand than supply for AI implementation

#### 3. Notion Template Market
- Real estate = top-9 most profitable Notion niche
- Bundle strategy ($79-129) outperforms singles 5-10x
- Top sellers earn $3K-10K/month in real estate niche
- 30K+ Singapore property agents = addressable market

#### 4. Wilson's Unfair Advantage
- Singapore real estate domain expertise
- Direct access to 30K+ agent network
- 73% less marketing spend vs. generalist competitors
