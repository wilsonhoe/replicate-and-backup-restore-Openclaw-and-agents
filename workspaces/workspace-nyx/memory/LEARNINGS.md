## 2026-04-10 - Vertical Specialization Wins Over Horizontal

**Finding:** In Singapore's AI market, vertical-specific tools and services consistently outperform horizontal offerings.

**Source:** AIMPACT, &7 Blog, ABUZZ launch, PxlPeak pricing analysis

**Key Evidence:**
- ABUZZ launched as generic AI VA agency for Singapore SMEs → adds competitive pressure to horizontal play

**Application:** All future opportunity research should prioritize vertical-specific positioning. Generic AI agency space is commoditizing; Singapore RE specialization is the moat.

## 2026-04-10 - PSG Grant Creates Both Opportunity and Margin Risk

**Finding:** Singapore's PSG grant (50-80% subsidy, up to S$30K) is a double-edged sword for AI agencies.

**Source:** &7 Blog, Singapore Budget 2026

**Key Evidence:**
- PSG makes SMEs willing to adopt (reduces sales friction)
- But SMEs know subsidies exist → price sensitivity increases
- Agencies must price knowing the client sees only 20-50% of sticker price
- Implementation gap (48/100 Pertama score) means SMEs need help even with subsidies

**Application:** When presenting AI service opportunities, highlight PSG eligibility as a sales accelerator but factor in compressed agency margins. Productized packages at fixed price points work better than custom engagements.
